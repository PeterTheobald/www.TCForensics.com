Services

Digital Forensics, Data Recovery, Breach Response.

Digital Forensics

We are experienced working with attorneys and courts to find, preserve, analyze and present digital evidence from computers, cell phones and other digital devices.

Data Recovery

TC Forensics can recover your lost or deleted data. Whether you have a damaged hard drive or a rogue ex-employee we can find those files and restore them.

Data Breach Response

Has your private data been released to the wild? If a hacker or a simple error has exposed private information we can help determine the extent of what was exposed and how it happened. We will help you prepare an appropriate response and prevent another breach.
